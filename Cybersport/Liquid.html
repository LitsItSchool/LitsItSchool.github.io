Team Liquid is a multi-gaming e-sports organization, founded in 2000 in the Netherlands. Initially, the team was a clan for the StarCraft: Brood War game, in 2010 switching to StarCraft 2. At the end of 2012, the organization signed the Dota 2 squad, and in 2017 won the largest international Dota 2 tournament The 2017 International, with the largest for the whole history of eSports prize money.
