Our Work

Ninjas in Pajamas (NiP: Ninja in pajamas) is a Swedish e-sports organization founded in 1999. The project was closed in 2007, but in 2012 it continued to work again. Currently represented by Dota 2, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, League of Legends, Overwatch and Paladins.

In the early 2000s, NiP was one of the most successful in the discipline Counter-Strike, according to GotFrag. The founders were Tommy "Potti" Ingemarsson (Tommy Ingemarsson) and Emil "HeatoN" Christensen (Emil Christensen). Their first success was 1st place in the CPL World Championship 2001. We all played in a clan called [OF], which was a pure clan, but we added bullet and elatenta to our lineup. Klanleider in that team was weak in managing the team and not particularly interested in the game, so the team decided to start something on their own and Tommy Ingemarsson came up with NiP. All this was new to them and had to ask for help from tootzi in creating the site of the clan and others. After that, they began to expand and take to the team people who were not with us in the lan.

Tournament

The final day of the StarLadder i-League StarSeries Season 2 saw Swedish team NiP take on French team G2 Esports. It had been a brilliant tournament so far with both teams showing some great performances on route to the final. It was NiP who took the trophy though after winning the final match 2-0. Congratulations to NiP who pick up the trophy and $130,000 top prize, even with a stand in member in the team throughout the tournament.
