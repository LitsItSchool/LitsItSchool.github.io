Our Work

A young organization from Kazakhstan, AVANGAR, signed its first CS: GO discipline in July 2017. They became a mix, previously speaking under the tag LoG. The start was quite good - winning several small tournaments and the CIS Minor Championship - Boston 2018, which gave the team a chance to compete for the ELEAGUE Major: Boston 2018.

Tournament

In the simefinal of EPICENTER 2018, the team from the CIS Avangar lose the CIS Natus Vincere with a score of 2-0 (de_dust 11-16 and de_train 12-16).
