Our Work

The Danish team, previously speaking for SK Gaming. On June 29, 2016, the players ended contracts, and the five went to a free swim. From July 1, 2016, the team took the temporary name of Team X, and on August 26, 2016, players create the organization Heroic.

Win 1

Tournament

In the final of International Gaming League 2016 - Grand Finals, the team from the European Heroic won the Asian squad with a score of 3-1 (de_cobblestone 7-16, de_overpass 19-17, de_mirage 16-1 and de_dust 16-10).

Win 2

Tournament

In the final of Games Clash Masters 2018, the team from the European Heroic won the European Kinguin with a score of 2-1 (de_train 16-9, de_mirage 3-16 and de_nuke 16-7).

Win 3

Tournament

In the final of TOYOTA Master CS:GO Bangkok 2018, the team from the European Heroic won the Asian TYLOO with a score of 3-2 (de_dust 16-5, de_overpass 25-23, de_train 16-19, de_mirage 12-16 and de_inferno 16-13).
