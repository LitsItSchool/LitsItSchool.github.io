Faze Clan is an American professional organization esports, mostly known for its presence on the stage of Call of Duty. In 2016, a new era for Faze began when the organization bought the professional Counter-Strike: Global Offensive team. At this point, it was noted that Faze Clan is expanding in different directions. This movement is called Faze 2.0 from Faze members and fans. The Faze CS: GO team has become one of the most successful registries in the 2017/2018 season. Faze Clan made $ 5,938,628.18 of the tournament prizes alone.
