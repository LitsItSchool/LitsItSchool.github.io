Our Work

The Turkish team in the CS: GO discipline was created in January 2015. Since its inception, players have participated in a variety of small tournaments and were able to win two seasons of QuickShot Arena. The biggest victory was “gold” at CS: GO Champions League Season 3 in April 2016, but we haven’t been able to select major tournaments so far.

Win 1

Tournament

In the final of ASUS ROG Summer 2016, the team from the European Space Soldiers won the European squad with a score of 2-0 (de_cache 16-11 and de_cobblestone 16-5).

Win 2

Tournament

In the final of GeForce Cup 2017, the team from the European Space Soldiers won the European squad with a score of 2-0 (de_cobblestone 16-8 and de_mirage 16-1).

Win 3

Tournament

In the final of DreamHack Open Austin 2018, the team from the European Space Soldiers won the North America squad with a score of 2-1 (de_mirage 16-8, de_overpass 5-16 and de_inferno 16-2).
