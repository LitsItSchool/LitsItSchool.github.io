Our Work

The first part of the G2 Esports was international. It includes players such as ScreaM, foxj, fox, dennis, rain and Maikelele. At that time, the team was called Gamers2. The perspective composition on paper has caused a lot of rumors and discussions: can the team take its rightful place on the CS: GO scene? Soon, ScreaM left the team and went to Titan, and in October 2015 there was a rebranding. Since then, the tag has changed to G2 Esports. The international team was remembered by the 3-4 place on DreamHack Open Cluj-Napoca 2015 in November 2015, and in January 2016 moved to FaZe Clan. For this reshuffle was paid $ 700,000. A few days later, a new roster was announced - the former Titan team five. Among them was ScreaM, which once began the history of the former team.

Win 1

Tournament

In the final of Esports Championship Series Season 1 - Finals, the team from the European G2 won the North American Luminosity with a score of 2-0 (de_dust 16-11 and de_overpass 16-5).

Win 2

Tournament

In the final of ESL Pro League Season 5: Finals, the team from the European G2 won the European North with a score of 3-1 (de_cache 16-13, de_cobblestone 16-5, de_overpass 14-16 and de_inferno 16-3).

Win 3

Tournament

In the final of DreamHack Masters Malmö 2017, the team from the European G2 won the European North with a score of 2-0 (de_inferno 16-12 and de_cobblestone 16-5).
