Our Work

In August 2012, mouz announced the British CS: GO squad, which included Mx, RattlesnK, Hughsy, RE1EASE and pt. In October, Team xXx was signed: approx, nex, stavros, strux1 and Troubley. In December, the British left the organization, and karrigan and zonixx replaced stavros and strux1. After the changes that took place in 2013, the squad did not settle down: in the cadiaN and cLy team, they replaced crisby and gob b. Then the composition continued to shake, some players were replaced by others. The latest changes occurred in December 2015, when Spiidi changed gob b. After that, the team managed to stabilize the roster, but to show a good result on the pro scene for them is still not an easy task.

Win 1

Tournament

In the final of StarLadder & i-League StarSeries Season 4, the team from the European Mousesports won the CIS Natus Vincere with a score of 2-1 (de_overpass 7-16, de_mirage 22-18 and de_train 16-9).

Win 2

Tournament

In the heavy final of V4 Future Sports Festival, the team from the European Mousesports won the European squad with a score of 2-1 (de_mirage 16-8, de_cobblestone 2-16 and de_train 16-14).

Win 3

Tournament

In the very heavy final of ESL One: New York 2018, the team from the European Mousesports won the North American Liquid with a score of 3-2 (de_cache 16-12, de_nuke 7-16, de_inferno 10-16, de_dust 19-17 and de_mirage 16-8).
